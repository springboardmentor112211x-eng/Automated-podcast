System Architecture & Flow

  • 1. Ingestion: Secure upload of raw podcast audio (MP3/WAV).
  • 2. ASR Engine: Full-stream transcription using Whisper Turbo.
  • 3. Logic Layer: LLM-assisted topic segmentation and summarization.
  • 4. Responsibility: Safety filters and confidence-based fallbacks.
  • 5. Human Review: Manual verification of transcripts before PDF export.
